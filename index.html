Hi, I’m Craig Gent. I’m a writer, researcher and journalist based in Leeds, Yorkshire.

Digital society, labour and power

I’m a scholar of digital media and culture, and the author of Cyberboss: The Rise of Algorithmic Management and the New Struggle for Control at Work (Verso Books).

Media, culture and politics

I have over ten years’ experience as a media practitioner. For eleven years, I was a senior editor and journalist at Novara Media, where I was a director.
